Accessibility statement

Last updated: 8 February 2026

Accessibility intent

Elysium By Georgie Rose aims to keep this website clear, readable and usable for as many visitors as possible across mobile and desktop devices.

  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and visible focus states.
  • Readable typography and spacing across core pages.
  • Colour choices designed for clear text contrast.

Known limitations

Third-party embeds (including Instagram and TikTok) may not always match the accessibility support of the core site. When embeds fail, fallback content and direct social links are provided.

Need help or spotted an issue?

If any part of this website is difficult to use, or if you need content in an alternative format, please get in touch and support will be provided as quickly as possible.
