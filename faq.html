Permanent jewellery

What is permanent jewellery?

Permanent jewellery is custom-fitted and welded on, so there is no clasp and it stays with you.

Does the welding process hurt?

No. The weld is quick and precise, and your skin is protected throughout.

Can permanent jewellery be removed?

Yes. If needed, you can cut the jump ring and keep the chain for re-welding.

How long does it last?

With proper care, your jewellery can last for years. We share aftercare tips at every fitting.

Materials

What metals do you offer?

We offer sterling silver, gold filled, and solid gold chains depending on availability.

Is gold filled safe for sensitive skin?

Gold filled jewellery is generally suitable for many people, but sensitivities vary. Let us know if you have concerns.

Do you offer charms and connectors?

Yes, you can add charms, initials, or connector pieces to personalise your chain.

Can I bring my own chain?

We usually work with our own chains to ensure welding quality. Ask us if you have a special request.

Appointments

How do I book?

Use the enquiry form or message us on Instagram. We will confirm available dates and times.

How long does an appointment take?

Most fittings take 15 to 30 minutes depending on the piece and any add-ons.

Can I book with a friend?

Yes, group appointments are welcome. Let us know your group size.

Where are appointments held?

Appointments can be arranged in Newcastle-under-Lyme or at partner venues.

Weddings

Do you offer wedding packages?

Yes. We have curated packages for bridal parties and guest experience stations.

Can the station match my wedding aesthetic?

We can style chains, charms, and displays to complement your wedding theme.

How many guests can you accommodate?

Packages scale from small bridal parties to full guest lists.

Do you require a deposit?

We typically confirm dates with a deposit. Details are shared during enquiry.

Pop-ups

What is a pop-up?

A pop-up is a temporary setup at a partner venue where guests can book fittings.

Do you travel for pop-ups?

Yes, we can travel for approved pop-ups. Travel fees may apply.

How do I host a pop-up?

Send venue details and preferred dates, and we will confirm availability.

Can pop-ups be private events?

Yes, we can host private group sessions at homes or hired venues.

Aftercare

How do I clean my jewellery?

Use a gentle jewellery cloth or mild soap and water. Dry thoroughly.

Can I wear it in the shower?

Yes, but avoid harsh chemicals and remove before applying lotions or perfumes.

What if it breaks?

Contact us and we can re-weld or repair depending on the issue.

What about medical scans?

Let your healthcare provider know. We can remove and re-weld if needed.

Send a message with your question and we will reply as soon as possible.
