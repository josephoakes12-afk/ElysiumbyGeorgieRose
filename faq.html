FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Short answers on appointments, treatments and aftercare.

Nails
How often should I book BIAB maintenance?

Most clients return every few weeks depending on growth and lifestyle.

Can I switch between BIAB and gel polish?

Yes. You can discuss the best plan at each appointment based on your nails and preferred finish.

Do you remove previous product?

Removal and prep options can be discussed when booking so enough time is scheduled.

Permanent Jewellery
Is permanent jewellery safe for daily wear?

It is designed for daily life, with care advice shared after fitting.

Can I get matching bracelets with a friend?

Yes. Pair and group bookings are welcome for matching pieces.

What if I need it removed later?

The joining point can be clipped safely and the chain can be kept for re-welding.
