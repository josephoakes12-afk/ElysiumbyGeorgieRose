BIAB

Builder in a bottle designed to reinforce natural nails while keeping a refined finish.

Best for

  • Clients wanting added strength with a natural shape
  • Regular maintenance appointments every few weeks
  • A polished look with or without nail art accents

Aftercare

  • Apply cuticle oil daily to keep the area hydrated
  • Use gloves for cleaning and prolonged water exposure
  • Book infills before lifting appears for best retention
Gel Polish

Glossy colour application with careful prep and tidy cuticle detailing.

Best for

  • Short to medium nails with a clean, high-shine finish
  • Clients who like regular colour changes
  • Special occasion sets and seasonal shades

Aftercare

  • Avoid peeling product and book professional removal
  • Use a nourishing hand cream after washing hands
  • Keep nails dry after showers before styling products
Gel Extensions

Structured extensions tailored to your preferred length, shape and finish.

Best for

  • Clients wanting extra length and structure
  • Statement designs, intricate art and special events
  • Creating a consistent shape across all nails

Aftercare

  • Avoid using nails as tools to reduce stress on the tip
  • Book maintenance when regrowth becomes noticeable
  • Follow aftercare guidance provided at your appointment
