How it works

A polished process from chain selection to aftercare.

Step 01 Choose your chain

Select a style and tone that suits your look and preferred finish.

Step 02 Custom fitting

Your piece is measured to the exact fit you prefer for comfort and finish.

Step 03 Precision welding

The chain is welded closed for a seamless clasp-free result.

Step 04 Aftercare guidance

You leave with practical guidance for care, maintenance and removal if ever needed.
