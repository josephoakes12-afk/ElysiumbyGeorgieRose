FAQs

Quick answers before you book.

Nails
How long do nail appointments usually take?

Timings vary by service, though most appointments are between 60 and 120 minutes.

Can I book BIAB if I prefer a natural look?

Yes. BIAB can be finished with a clean, minimal look or paired with colour and design.

What prep should I do before my nail appointment?

Please arrive with clean nails and mention any existing product during your booking enquiry.

Permanent Jewellery
How does welded jewellery work?

Your chain is measured, fitted, then welded closed for a seamless finish.

Can jewellery be removed if needed?

Yes. The connecting point can be clipped and the chain saved for re-welding at a later appointment.

Do you offer event and pop-up bookings?

Yes. Pop-ups and private events are available, including wedding keepsake experiences.

Booking and aftercare
How do I book?

Use the contact form or message directly on Instagram with your preferred service and dates.

Will I receive aftercare guidance?

Yes. Aftercare pointers are shared at each appointment for both nails and jewellery.

Can I ask questions before confirming?

Absolutely. Enquiries are welcome before booking so you can choose the right service.
