FAQs

Quick answers to common questions.

Does permanent jewellery hurt to remove?

No. If you ever need to remove your jewellery, simply cut the jump ring with scissors and keep the chain for re-welding.

How long does the appointment take?

Most fittings take 15 to 30 minutes depending on the piece and any charms.

Can I choose different metals?

Yes. Options include sterling silver, gold filled, and solid gold chains.

Are pop-ups available for groups?

Absolutely. We can bring the experience to salons, boutiques, or private parties.
