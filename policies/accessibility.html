Accessibility statement

Last updated: 8 February 2026

Accessibility approach

  • Semantic headings and landmarks across key pages.
  • Keyboard navigation support and visible focus states.
  • Reduced motion support for visitors who prefer less animation.
  • Responsive layouts optimised for mobile and desktop.

Known limitations

Some third-party embeds may not provide the same accessibility support as the core site content. Fallback content is provided where practical.
